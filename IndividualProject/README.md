Dodge The Buildings VR — GAME MANUAL

Abhay Bhullar | CMPT 461 | A-Frame WebVR

OVERVIEW
Abhay Dodge VR is a first-person VR dodge game. Buildings rush toward you down an endless city street. Tilt your head left or right to steer out of their path. Survive as long as possible and beat your high score.

SETUP & INSTALLATION
Option A — Open directly (simplest):

Save index.html to your computer
Double-click the file to open it in your browser
Click the screen to start

Option B — Shareable link:

Click the given link below to play game
https://bhullar07.github.io/


CONTROLS

Start game: Click anywhere on the splash screen
Move left: Turn head left (more than 6 degrees)
Move right: Turn head right (more than 6 degrees)
Look around: Move mouse (desktop) or rotate phone (mobile/VR)
Enter VR mode: Click the goggles icon at the bottom-right of the screen
Exit VR mode: Press Esc on desktop, or use your headset's back button

Your character runs forward automatically. Steer only by looking left or right.

LEVELS
Level 1 — Starts at score 0, obstacle speed 0.10
Level 2 — Starts at score 1000, obstacle speed 0.18
Level 3 — Starts at score 2500, obstacle speed 0.26
Levels advance automatically as your score increases.

SCORING
Your current score is displayed in the top-left of your view at all times. When the game ends your score and all-time high score are shown. The high score is saved automatically in your browser between sessions.

AUDIO
Sound is generated using the Web Audio API — no audio files needed. A chiptune melody plays during the game and a crash sound plays on collision. Audio starts when you click the splash screen (browsers require a click before playing sound). If you hear nothing, check that your device is not muted.
